Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of defending our computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks, sometimes called unauthorized access. As technology evolves in this modern era, security becomes one of the biggest threats to mankind, particularly in the era of big data and IoT.
