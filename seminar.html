Arduino Seminar Overview

On Tuesday 16th May 2023, we were visited by guest lecturer Ali Ulker from Dogu Collage for a seminar on Arduino. During this seminar, we learned a lot about it. We had a presentation and got hands-on experience with the Arduino kit, which consisted of a breadboard, jump wires, resistors, and accessories like LED lights and water sensors that work with water or wet sensor triggers. We assembled all these parts together for a practical session to see how each one of them works. It was a great experience!

Here are some key points from the seminar:

  • Arduino can be used to perform any function based on how well one can program it.
  • There are additional features and applications of Arduino beyond what was covered in the seminar.
  • The seminar instructor shared what he usually teaches the students at lower grades, as they use a different platform, while we used the Arduino studio, which is more complex and suitable for adults.
  • Regarding where to get Arduino parts, the professor mentioned that they mainly sourced them from Turkey, but they are exploring alternatives and will provide means to buy them if needed.
